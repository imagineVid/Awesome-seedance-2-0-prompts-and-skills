# Seedance 2.0 research note

## Positioning

Seedance 2.0 is ByteDance Seed's unified multimodal audio-video model for generation, reference-guided creation, and editing. Its strongest workflows assign explicit roles to image, video, and audio inputs, then direct camera movement, performance, continuity, and synchronized sound as one production brief.

## Curation method

Community cases were discovered with the local twitterapi.io workflow on 2026-07-18, then screened for explicit model evidence, complete prompt text, source attribution, visible result media, uniqueness, and production value. Raw selected records are retained in `data/research/` for auditability.
